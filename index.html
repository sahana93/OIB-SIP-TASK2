1931-2015

Biography of Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam became the former President of India and received the title of Indian Scholar. He is also referred to as the Indian Rocketeer due to his involvement in India's rocket and space programs. Take this test for your doctor. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam is watching. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam's full name is Awul Pakir Jainulabdin Abdul Kalam. Awul Pakir Jainulabdin Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931 to a Tamil Muslim family at the Rameshwaram pilgrimage center on Pamban Island, the then President of Madras, and now the state of Tamil Nadu.
